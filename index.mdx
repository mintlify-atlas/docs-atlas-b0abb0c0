YT Downloader is a modern Windows desktop application for downloading YouTube content. Built with WinUI 3 and C#, it gives you a clean, native interface to save videos in MP4, extract audio as MP3, and batch-download entire playlists — all without ads, accounts, or subscriptions.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install YT Downloader">
    Download the latest `YT-Downloader.Setup.exe` from the [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/TXG0Fk3/YT-Downloader/releases) page and run the installer, or use [Scoop](/installation#scoop).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste a YouTube URL">
    Click the **+** button in the app, paste any YouTube video or playlist URL, and hit **Search**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose format and quality">
    Select your preferred resolution (for video) or confirm MP3 (for audio), then pick a save location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download">
    Click **Download** and watch the progress bar. Your file is saved and ready when the status shows **Completed**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  YT Downloader requires **Windows 10 (build 17763)** or later and is available for both **x64** and **ARM64** processors.
</Note>
