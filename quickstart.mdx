---
title: "Quick start"
description: "Download your first YouTube video or audio file in minutes"
---

<Note>
  YT Downloader uses FFmpeg to mux video and audio streams and to convert audio to MP3. FFmpeg is bundled with the installer — no manual setup required.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open YT Downloader">
    Open **YT Downloader** from the **Start Menu**. The app launches and shows your download queue, which is empty to start.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the download dialog">
    Click the **+** button in the app to open the download dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste a YouTube URL">
    Paste a YouTube video or playlist URL into the URL field. The app accepts standard `youtube.com` URLs. The app fetches the video metadata and available stream options.

    Example video URL:
    ```
    https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
    ```

    Example playlist URL:
    ```
    https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
    ```

    <Note>
      The app accepts URLs in the form `youtube.com/watch?v=...` for videos and `youtube.com/playlist?list=...` for playlists. Short `youtu.be` links are not supported.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose format and quality">
    Select the format and quality for the download.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Video (MP4)">
        Select a quality level such as **1080p**, **720p**, or **480p**. The download will be saved as an MP4 file with video and audio muxed together by FFmpeg.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Audio (MP3)">
        Select **MP3** to extract audio only. The app picks the highest-bitrate audio stream and converts it to MP3 at 192 kbps.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a save location">
    Select a folder to save the file. If you have **Always ask where to save** enabled in settings, a folder picker dialog opens. Otherwise, the file is saved to your default downloads folder (`%USERPROFILE%\Downloads`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the download">
    Click **Download**. The item appears in the download queue with a progress indicator. Up to 3 downloads can run in parallel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your file">
    When the status shows **Completed**, your file is ready in the folder you selected. For playlist downloads, each video is saved as a separate file inside a dedicated subfolder.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Playlist downloads can take significantly longer than single video downloads, depending on the number of videos and your internet connection. The app fetches metadata for all playlist videos before queuing them, then downloads up to 3 at a time.
</Tip>
